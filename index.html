Why us?

We tell you

From big to small businesses the importance of organization is a key to success. Keep track of their employee’s availability is important and having that information in a handy way and anytime is essential. Most small and medium business do not have a good method to organize schedule for their employees during the week and sometimes it can lead to miss shifts and waste money and time. So, as a team we have come up with an app that is going to give those employers access to keep track of every employee’s availability and make any change from the confidence of their smartphone in a private way.
